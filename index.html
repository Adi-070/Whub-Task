DASS


The DASS is a 21 item self-report questionnaire aimed to measure the three related negative emotional states of depression, anxiety and stress. Please read each statement and mark a number 0, 1, 2 or 3 which indicates how much the statement applied to you over the past week. There are no right or wrong answers. Do not spend too much time on any statement.
